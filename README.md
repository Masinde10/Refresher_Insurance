### BUSINESS UNDERSTANDING
## Project Overview
One of the basic needs of a human being is access to emdical care. Everyday we have people dying to the inability to access affordable medical attention. Insurance is key in ensuring that everybody is cattered for healthwise. In this project we are going to explore the factore that affect the insurance fees for different patients.

## Problem statement
In our society, many people lack the access to proper medical care because of financial constraints. In cases where insurance is already working, it favors a small portion, the employed. Healthcare should be accessible to everyone since it is a basic need.

## Stakeholders
The main stakeholders of this project are healthcare providers, Insurance firms and the society at large who often are patients at different hospitals. Insurance firms will benefit from it in that they can learn how to provide cheap insurance while still hitting their profit margins. The patients will benefit from  it by learning what affects their insurance costs and make better decisions.
